I am now studying Information Management and Technology which is a lot of fun and I absolutely love my major. My favorite class I've taken throughout college has been IST 344 with Professor Young. From that class, I've learnt a lot about presenting and reporting and now feel that I'm able to give presentations in all my class without feeling nervous and feeling confident about what I'm going to present. I enjoy my coursework throughout college and am excited to see what other classes I'll be taking in the future!

Around the age of 12 I had started to get very interested in playing tennis. Throughout high school, majority of my time was spent playing tennis everyday after school and playing in tournaments during the weekend. On my highschool team I played varsity doubles my sophomore and junior year and varisty singles my senior year. I had a lot of fun playing tennis and had decided that I wanted to start coaching. Around the age of 16, I started teaching kids from the age of 5 to 12 how to play. It was always a really great rewarding feeling knowing I was making a difference even in the slightest to their playing. To this day I still love to play tennis, but my biggest regret is that I do not get to play as often as I'd like to.
