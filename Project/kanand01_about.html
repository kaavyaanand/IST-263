Bio

My name's Kaavya Anand. I am a current junior in the iSchool at Syracuse University studying Information Management and Technology. I was born on February 19th 1999 in Bryn Mawr, PA. I am from Malvern, PA and have lived there for majority of my life.

I have two loving parents, a brother, and a sweet puppy named Leila. I am currently very involved in my school work and do not have much free time to do other things. I am a part of BeIT, Alpha Phi, JPC, and recently just volunteered for a company called OurWayv.


Right now I am looking for an internship for the upcoming summer and hope to find one very soon! This past summer, I worked as an IT intern at Paratek Pharmaceuticals in King of Prussia, PA.

I am not quite sure what I want to do in my future or what I want to concetrate in in the iSchoool, but I know that no matter what I choose I can't go wrong.



School


I am currently studying Information Management and Technology which is a lot of fun and I absolutely love my major. I find that I do not have much difficulty in any of my classes and that I do a good job of really learning the material. My favorite class I've taken throughout college has been IST 344 with Professor Young. From that class, I've learnt a lot about presenting and reporting and now feel that I'm able to give presentations in all my class without feeling nervous and feeling confident about what I'm going to present.


One class I have had the most difficulty in was IST 346, Information Technology Management and Administration. Throughout the class I had a very hard time really understanding what was going on, but thankfully had many different friends who were always there to help me. I enjoy my coursework throughout college and am excited to see what other classes I'll be taking in the future!



Hobbies



One of my favorite hobbies growing up was musical instruments. From the age of 5 I took violin lessons and self taught myself the piano. I also played the drums and the oboe for some time too! I played in my schools orchestra and band until I was 14. By the time I was in High School my interest in music had started to drift.



When I was in middle school I started playing tennis. By the time I was 14 I was playing for my High School team and developed a serious interest in it. I started playing tennis nearly everyday for hours and competing in tournaments on the weekend. I played varsity doubles my sophomore and junior year and varsity singles my senior year. I still love playing tennis to this day and know I will continue to love to play for the rest of my life!
